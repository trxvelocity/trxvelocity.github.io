TRX Velocity Plugin comes bundled with crypto transaction screening and monitoring capabilities. Once enabled, it empowers your platform to monitor cryptocurrency transactions in real time, uncovering links to suspicious activities such as money laundering, terrorist financing, dealings with sanctioned entities, and other illicit activities. This ensures your application remains compliant with global financial regulations while enhancing trust and security for your users.

Our transaction monitoring solution is powered by industry-leading platforms like Chainalysis, Elliptic and GraphSense, delivering comprehensive coverage and accuracy. Each transaction is screened against up-to-date blockchain intelligence, enabling you to detect high-risk wallets, suspicious patterns, and flagged addresses. Whether youre processing deposits, withdrawals, or transfers, the plugin helps identify risks before they impact your operations, safeguarding your platform from potential legal and reputational issues.

The screening process leverages blockchain analytics and machine learning to analyze transaction patterns and wallet interactions. By cross-referencing addresses against global watchlists and analyzing behavioral data, the TRX Velocity Plugin delivers actionable insights directly within your application. With this tool, you can proactively mitigate risks, streamline compliance workflows, and maintain a high standard of integrity in your platforms financial ecosystem.
